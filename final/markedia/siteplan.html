Site Plan

Site Name : Markedia

Website of a digital marketing agency which offers full-service of web marketing and conversion to boost customer growth.

Site purpose

This website will promote a virtual company in digital marketing that aims to offer services worldwide. It serves to display the information related to the company services and solutions, contact and form to quotations. It also contains funnels to collect data through popups and forms for further marketing purposes.

I chose this topic because I would like to apply all the lessons learned in this course to a project related to my domain of predilection which is digital creation. From modern html and css development, to javascript modularization and data fetching, I will practice all along this project development. It will be a good purpose for applying and testing accessibility, performance and other web quality recommandations.

Scenarios

Who are the main audience of the project?

This virtual digital market targets specific audiences, those who have interests in promoting their services and products on the web and social networks.

What services the Markedia virtual agency will offer?

There are various digital services such as: digital marketing, ecommerce and content marketing, design and web development, social media marketing.

Through what means does the website use to collect customer informations?

Forms (quotation and contact forms), popup (newsletter), localStorage and cookies.

Color Schemas

Primary color: blue (00bbf9)
The primary color will be used in logo, main headings such as h2, buttons

Secondary color: yellow (#fee440)
The secondary color will be used in some part of logo, secondary headings such as h3 and block background.
Tertiary color: pink (#f15bb5)
The tertiary color will be used in some part of logo as well, in button hover, and some links
Accent color 1: violet (#9b5de5)
To accent some button and link backgrounds, some title and block
Accent color 2 : green (#00f5d4)
To accent some button and link backgrounds on hover
